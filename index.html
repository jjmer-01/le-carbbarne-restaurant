Our Story

A fresh start

Gabrielle and Jib Wahlberg have been in the kitchen since their Grandfather, Lowell, first gave them carte blanche to garnish his epic Thanksgiving dinners. The food he passed on to his grandkids translated into a life-long love affair with the gastronomic arts.

During a break in Jib's employment after an economic downturn, they realized they were both craving a creative challenge. From there, the concept of Le Carbbarné was born.

Break away from boring

Gabrielle's travels during University transformed her perception of what was possible with food. She loves to borrow from traditional Mexican and Thai cuisine. Jib's unparalleled experience working in 5-star Michelin restaurants during his young adulthood shaped his unrelenting commitment to service.
